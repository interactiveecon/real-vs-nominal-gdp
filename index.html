Nominal vs Real GDP

Adjust prices and quantities for two goods over three years. The lab computes Nominal GDP, Real GDP (base-year prices), and the GDP Deflator. Your goal: identify whether changes are driven by prices, quantities, or both.

Key formulas
  • Nominal GDP in year t: \(\sum_i P_{i,t}Q_{i,t}\)
  • Real GDP (base year b) in year t: \(\sum_i P_{i,b}Q_{i,t}\)
  • GDP Deflator: \(\frac{\text{Nominal}}{\text{Real}}\times 100\)
